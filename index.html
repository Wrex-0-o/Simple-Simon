The Simon Game

Similar to the Drum Kit Project, the Simon Game project is built by me, as a test to learn my current skills at writting JS code, while making concious decisions regarding the logic behind the working of the Game.

Here are the rules to the game, I am going to explain them in the JJK fashion:

  • The Player upon entering the page must use their cursed energy to activate the system.
  • The system's response to the stimuli would be to throw cursed buttons at them.
  • There are total four buttons - each embedded with a different curse.
  • The Player then must use reverse curse technique to follow up with the system buttons.
  • Or else, the Player would be striped off their cursed technique and rendered useless for the rest of eternity.
